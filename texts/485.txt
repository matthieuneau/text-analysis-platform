age : 2 - 5 years little ones can make flowers using muffin liners, glitter glue and buttons. what you need : • glue ( we used glitter glue ) • muffin liners ( we used some plain and some silver ) what you do : - let your little ones put glue on the construction paper whereever they would like to put a " flower ". - have them place a muffin liner on the glue. repeat. - encourage your child / student to put a dab of glue in the center of the muffin liner. - then, have them place a button on the glue dab. - to complete the picture, little ones can use markers to draw stems and leaves on the construction paper. www. kinderart. com | www. kinderartlittles. com © andrea mulder - slater and kinderart ®
