description from flora of china lianas large, herbaceous or woody. inflorescences extra - axillary, umbel - like or short racemelike, occasionally branched, shorter than leaves, usually few flowered. flowers large. calyx without glands. corolla subrotate ; lobes overlapping to right. corona shallowly cupular, inserted at base of gynostegium, fleshy, apex entire or shallowly undulate. filaments connate ; anthers very large, decurrent to base of column, apex acuminate ; pollinia 2 per pollinarium, slender, falcate, pendulous. stigma head scarcely umbonate. follicles stout, cylindric - fusiform, slightly tapering to a blunt apex. two species : china, india, myanmar, sri lanka, thailand ; one species in china.
